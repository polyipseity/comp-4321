Exquisite!, 31 March 1999
Author:
DeeDee-10 from San Francisco
Gary Oldman never fails to turn in brilliant performances time after time,
and with the magnificent cast of Immortal Beloved he once again proves he is
one of the most fascinating actors of our time. I simply could not break
myself away from the video; it was so spellbinding. The intimacy of the
actors in their relationship with each other was very moving. Everyone
brought intensity, passion, warmth, desperation, frustration, etc. It was
all there with such believability. The last scene with Beethoven and Johanna
Reiss was heart wrenching. The expression on Oldman's face as he realized
his fate simply reduced me to tears. His pain juxtaposed with Steege's
internalized passivity was one of the finest pieces of acting I have seen in
a long time. And, the music!
