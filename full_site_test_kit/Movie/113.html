Astonishing., 26 June 2002
Author:
Jimmy Green from Ipswich, UK
This is without a doubt one of the greatest documentaries that I have ever
seen. The narration is easy to follow, not too self-consciously obtuse or
referential to previous films, and the piecing together is simply masterful.
The only quibble is the lack of information on Bruce's early life.
Things to love about this film:* the Game of Death sequence, fully restored, edited and soundtracked to
Bruce's wishes (according to recently found production
notes)* The one finger (and a thumb) pressups - although rather
short* The one inch punch
Truly awesome and 10/10.
