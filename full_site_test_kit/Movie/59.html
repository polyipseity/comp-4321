Wong Jing take on Sex and the City, 5 June 2004
Author:
goodmen from Canada
The Wong Jing direct, produce and write is back with his version of the
popular HBO series, 'Sex and the City'. The stars power in this movie
is great, we have Carina Lau has Selina the lead actress who is a
psychoanalyst, who hang out with her friends (Athena Chu has a erotic
story for woman, Yoyo Mung has a multi-restaurant owner who like the
danger side, and Pinky Cheung whose dad is a triad boss goes retirement
and know how to fight). The story begin when Selina ex came to her and
ask her to take care of her wild daughter (Cecilia Cheung in another
bizarre look) and tried to cure her wild behaviour. Well the rest of
the storyline consist of the 4 woman and there relationship with the
guys, we have Tony Leung Ka Fai going after Carina Lau, Athena Chu
going after Edisen Chen, Picky Cheung going after Andy Hui, and Lu Yi
going after Yoyo Mung. The movie was mildly entertaining and the stars in this movie were fun
to watch, but the flow and situation of the story do get tiring. Andy
Hui look hot with his bodyguard suit and his non facial expression.
Some of the stuff in this movie is just a recycle from other movies
(Analysis This for one), which make the movie rather less enjoyable.
The narrative talk from Carina was excitement giving us enough
information on her friends and fine performance from everyone except
Edisen Chen (I don't hate this fellow Canadian). This movie lack new
idea, and the comedy is not that funny, I can't really compare to the
HBO series because I never watched it.If you are fans of any of these stars I recommend watching this because
the movie wasn't boring at all. Don't expect the movie to be some
clever script or anything special.
