there's no film like it!, 27 October 1999
Author:
Anu kapoor (tandon@tandon.screaming.net) from London, england
Mann is very good. I thought it would be a remake of Titanic and it was in
the first half a bit like it but in the second half it was totally
different. Aamir Khan was good and played the charming hero, Manisha
Koirala
was fantastic and Anil Kapoor was excellent!The film is about Dev Karan Singh (Aamir Khan), who is a womanizer who is
already engaged to some rich brat but still goes after the woman but never
actually loves any of them until one day he goes on a cruise and meets a
girl he's crazy about Priya (Manisha Koirala), who won in a fancy dress
contest to go on a cruise. Priya and dev meet and dev hits on her the first
time he sees her and later she gets to love him too and goes along with him
to meet his grandma (Sharmila Tagore). The two reveal they're both getting
engaged and vow to break of their marriages and meet at the gateway of
india
on valentine's day and Priya says to Dev to make something of himself and
stand on his own two feet. At the airport their partners to be arrive to
pick them up and Priya's alliance turns out to be Anil Kapoor and Dev's
alliance turns out to be someone who i don't know the name of and there the
two separate.Dev finally breaks off his marriage and becomes poor and makes a living by
painting and as for Priya she doesn't have the heart to break to break off
her marriage but afterwards he finds out and is fine with it and he helps
her get ready for valentine's day. When the day comes the two are on their
way but something happens to Priya...........Mann is good dispite it was a flop in India i think i may be wrong but i
think it was. Mann deserves a 9.9 out of 10.
