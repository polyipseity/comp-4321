The harm it can cause if you swap places with your twin, 11 September 1999
Author:
Anu kapoor (tandon@tandon.screaming.net) from London, england
This film is about a wealthy couple and the woman is giving birth and stop
by a gypsie's house and by the next morning the woman has twins but only
thinks she's got one because the gypsie took one of them to bring up on her
own and so later on the two twins somehow got themselves in a knot and end
up swaping places. Dharmendra and Sanjeev kumar play the heros. this film
has it's fair share of comedy and drama.
